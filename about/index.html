About Cashpo

About CashPo

In the era of an increasingly complex economy, CashPo is your ideal finance partner. We are here to revolutionize the way people view credit processing. CashPo is an innovative platform where you can avail effortless and seamless credits through our app.

CashPo is a Fintech organization operated by Intellimint Technologies Pvt. Ltd. since 2020. We aim to provide quick, reliable, and secure personal loans to salaried employees. We stand strongly by our principles to make the loan process easier and accessible to all. Whether you’re struggling with a personal financial crisis, medical bill payment, children’s fee payment, or just need some extra cash in hand for a ceremony, we’ve got you covered. With us, you can ensure a secure life for you and your loved ones.

What We Offer?

Gone are those days when you had to go back and forth to banks for getting your loan processed. With CashPo, you can get a loan on the go!

CashPo facilitates short term and long term personal loans with our chain of several non-banking financial institutions. Additionally, we address creditworthy clients through innovating the financial landscape. This means that apart from credit score based underwriting, we have deployed several forms and strategies of underwriting alternate data sources. This new technique allows us to evaluate your credit score through the non-generic methods and offer personal loans based on it.

We strive to fulfil our objective of inducing a paradigm shift in the dynamics of loan process and stand by our vision of ‘Sabke Paas Rupaiya’.

Our Vision

Our tagline ‘Sabke Paas Rupaiya’ (Money for all), speaks volumes about our vision. We are here to help you become financially secure and help them focus on growth. We believe in transparency in our processes, cultivating a strong relationship with our clients, and breaking the barriers of traditional banking for a better tomorrow.

We promise a brighter day, every day.

Our Mission

The culture at CashPo directs at security, growth, and independence. We aim at making loans easily accessible to all without any hassles. Our seamless loan process is designed to make loan application as easy as a walk in the park. With zero stress and a hundred percent safe, reliable, and flexible process, we empower your financial decisions!
